Operations KPI Dashboard — Solar and Battery Installation Co

An end-to-end Power BI dashboard built on a multi-table dataset spanning 300 projects, 250 sales leads, and a detailed cost breakdown across equipment, labor, and permits. The dashboard surfaces key operational metrics — revenue by state, gross margin, win rate by sales rep, battery attach rate, and cost composition — with interactive slicers enabling dynamic filtering by state, battery type, date range, and sales rep.

Tools: Power BI · DAX · Power Query
Focus: KPI Reporting · Sales Analytics · Cost Analysis
Context: Residential Solar · Operations

Unit Economics · Solar Operations

Job-Level Profitability Model — Solar and Battery Installation Co

Built from the ground up to give Solar and Battery Installation Co margin visibility at the individual job level — something that didn't exist before. The model integrates labor hours, material costs, and revenue recognition to surface true job-level profitability, enabling data-driven pricing decisions and clearer prioritization across the project portfolio. Also includes a cash flow forecasting layer to support liquidity planning in a project-based revenue environment.

Tools: Excel · Dynamic Forecasting
Focus: Unit Economics · Cash Flow
Context: Residential Solar · Operations

Financial Modeling · Agriculture · Portfolio Case Study

Crop Nursery Financial Model

A four-year integrated financial model for a specialty crop nursery, built on an accrual basis with FIFO inventory accounting across three distinct crop lines. The model tracks revenue, cost of goods sold, and gross profit at both the product and consolidated level — designed to support production planning and investment decisions for early-stage agricultural operations.

Tools: Excel
Horizon: 4-Year Model
Accounting: Accrual · FIFO Inventory
