About Me

Analyst. Architect.
Business Partner.

I'm a Senior Financial Analyst with 8+ years of experience building driver-based forecasting models, performance reporting frameworks, and scalable financial process infrastructure across regulated utilities, residential solar, and investment management.

My work sits at the intersection of finance and operations. I'm most effective when there's a gap between what the business knows and what leadership needs to see — whether that means redesigning a broken forecast tool, automating a manual reporting cycle, or building KPI dashboards that turn cost and operational data into executive-ready insights.

I'm a CFA Level III candidate with a Master's in International Business from Tübingen, including MBA exchanges in Japan and South Korea. I bring a genuinely global perspective — having lived across Europe, Asia, and North America — and thrive in environments that reward rigorous thinking, continuous improvement, and finance that actually drives decisions.
